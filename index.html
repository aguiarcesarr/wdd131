Pederneiras, Brazil

The name Pederneiras, a city in São Paulo, originates from the abundance of “firestone” (flint) found in the region, which was used to produce fire. The farm that gave rise to the city, called “Fazenda Pederneiras,” was named this way by the first settlers in 1848.
